# Bords Pricing System & Model

## Pricing Philosophy

We charge for:
- **Workspace value** (base tier)
- **Collaboration intensity** (collaborator limits)
- **AI usage** (scalable credits)

We do **not** charge per seat/user.

---

## Plans

### Free — $0/forever

**Target:** Individuals and small teams

- Unlimited boards
- 3 active collaborators per board
- 50 AI credits/month
- Public sharing
- Basic templates
- Offline-first sync

---

### Starter — $8/workspace/month

**Target:** Small teams getting organized

- 10 active collaborators
- 300 AI credits/month
- Basic automations
- Export features
- Private boards
- Priority email support

> Emerging market localized pricing: $5–$6/month (optional)

---

### Growth — $15/workspace/month ⭐ Most Popular

**Target:** Startups and active teams

- 25 active collaborators
- 1,000 AI credits/month
- Voice collaboration (Rooms)
- Advanced linking
- Workflow automation
- Everything in Starter

---

### Scale — $25/workspace/month

**Target:** Serious teams at full speed

- Unlimited collaborators
- Advanced analytics
- Admin controls
- Priority AI (faster responses)
- Version history
- Everything in Growth

## Upgrade Triggers

Users upgrade when they hit:
1. **Collaborator limit** — "Too many people on this board"
2. **AI limit** — "AI credits used up"
3. **Privacy need** — "Need private/internal workflows"
4. **Collaboration need** — "Need real-time voice collaboration"

These are designed as **friction moments, not hard walls**.

## Sales Funnels

### Funnel A: Self-Serve (Main Growth Engine)
1. User lands on site
2. Clicks "Start Free"
3. Creates board immediately
4. Invites others
5. Hits limits → upgrade

> No friction signup. No credit card required.

### Funnel B: Product-Led Expansion
1. Team grows usage
2. Uses AI heavily
3. Needs more control
4. Upgrades tiers organically

### Funnel C: Enterprise Conversion
1. Team uses Bords internally
2. Leadership notices
3. Needs security + control
4. Clicks "Book a Demo"
5. Demo → close on $299–$1,500+/month plan

---

## Emerging Market Strategy

**Problem:** $15/month is expensive in price-sensitive markets (e.g. Nigeria).

**Solution:**
- Localized pricing: Starter $5–$6, Growth $10–$12
- OR: Keep pricing + longer trials / more free AI credits

**Rule:** Never globally reduce pricing.
